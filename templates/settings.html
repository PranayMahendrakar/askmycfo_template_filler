What the AI does

After the rule engine runs, any rows in the extracted Excel that didn't match a pattern (e.g. "Cash credit from State Bank of India") get sent to your model along with the catalog of patterns. The model returns its best guess — for example, borrowings_current with confidence 0.92.

If the confidence is at or above your threshold and the target pattern has a CSV bucket, the raw label is appended to your bs_keywords.csv or pl_keywords.csv. Next time a report uses the same phrasing, the substring matcher catches it without calling OpenAI at all.

Anything below the threshold (or matched to a hand-curated pattern) shows up on the result page as a manual-review item.
