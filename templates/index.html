How it works

  1. Detect. Scans the PDF, finds the BS & P&L pages using title patterns and number-density scoring. Detects Standalone & Consolidated separately.
  2. Trim. Writes those pages into a small PDF so subsequent steps don't re-scan the whole report.
  3. Extract. Reads each table column-by-column (header-anchored, watermark-stripped) into a plain Excel — values exactly as they appear in the report, normalised to Lakhs.
  4. Apply rules. Walks the extracted rows, matches each one to the closest pattern from your catalog, runs the per-field arithmetic defined in Rules, and scales to your chosen units.
  5. (Optional) AI auto-fix. Any line item your dictionary doesn't recognise gets sent to OpenAI for classification; high-confidence suggestions are added to your CSV dictionary and the pipeline re-runs.
  6. Fill template. Writes the values into your Input_Templates.xlsx at the right rows; totals are Excel formulas so they stay live.
